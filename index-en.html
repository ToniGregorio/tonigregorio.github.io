TONI GREGÓRIO

Guitarist, Composer & Music Producer

About

With over 20 years of experience, Toni Gregório is a Brazilian guitarist, composer, and music producer. He studied Classical Guitar at UFRN and MPB/Jazz at the renowned Conservatório de Tatuí, combining academic knowledge with practical experience to create complete musical solutions. His work is internationally recognized, including awards at the Cannes Film Festival for the soundtrack of the film "A Tradicional Família Brasileira Katu" and the Prague Quadrennial.
