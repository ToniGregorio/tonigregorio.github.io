About Toni Gregório

Over two decades creating unique sonic experiences

I'm Toni Gregório, a guitarist, composer, and music producer with over 20 years of experience creating soundtracks and musical productions for film, theater, games, and independent artists.

With a Bachelor's degree in Classical Guitar and studies in MPB/Jazz at the renowned Tatuí Conservatory, I combine academic knowledge with practical experience to create complete musical solutions.
